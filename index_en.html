About

Usizo is a journal platform that can be used safely and easily by the therapist and the client, creating a much easier and faster connection between the two of them. Usizo was developed with the purpose of easing the work of the therapists and letting them do what is the most important thing: to treat their clients, while we take care of all the other things.

Shortly, we aim to cover the needs of both the client and therapists, by making the platform easy to use and taking care of other important things.

Features

Usizo journal has two type of portals: for the client and the therapists. Each of these platforms have the necessary features that covers the needs for both.

These are the following features that the platform for the therapist has:

Dashboard
Overview of status and notifications

Shared files
Store or share files and create services


People
All the clients along with their info

History
All actions done on the platform


Messages
The inbox-mail platform

Conversations
Secure video conversations


Courses
Create or add a client to event/course

Booking
Booking appointments in calendar
